UBC Engineering Design Teams

UBC Rocket

UBC Rocket is a student engineering design team at the University of British Columbia dedicated to the design, manufacture and launch of suborbital rockets.

UBC Steel Bridge

The UBC Steel Bridge Team is an extra-curricular club that welcomes all students to participate in the annual Steel Bridge Competition hosted by ASCE and AISC.

UBC BEST

The UBC Biomedical Engineering Student Team (BEST) develops context appropriate solutions to medical challenges worldwide through its 7 unique projects.

UBC Thunderbots

UBC Thunderbots is a student-driven engineering design team that designs, prototypes and builds autonomous soccer-playing robots to compete in the annual RoboCup conference.

UBC Mars Colony

The university's newest engineering team, UBC Mars Colony, is focused on developing technology to enable human settlement on Mars.
